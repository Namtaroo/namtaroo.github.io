BACKGROUND

I am an independent and passionate game developer currently based in the Philippines. Due to some circumstances, I am presently an undergraduate studying Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering at Partido State University, but my heart truly lies in game development. Although I found my passion late in life, my love for transforming ideas into unique experiences that bring joy and inspiration to others drove me to pursue this career. I am always enthusiastic about learning new things and meeting new awesome people. Also, I have already worked with an independent company for two (2) years in developing their projects as a generalist programmer, and I've been making games for game jams and hobby projects for almost five (5) years now.

In the future, I hope my dedication to game development would finally allow me to establish my very own independent studio and be able to create games that would eventually inspire more people to be involved in the process of giving extraordinary virtual experiences and continuously reaching greater milestones.
